SINGAPORE

Where traditional and modern meets is the best way to describe the country. Sky-high buildings and high-end shopping malls are what comes to most minds when we think of Singapore. However, the traditional side of the city-state is still very much present. This is why it’s so popular among foreign tourists and especially to Filipinos because you get to enjoy both sides of the country. Also, let’s not forget its clean and highly efficient subway system.

universal studios singapore.

A must go destination for everyone, be it for children or adults. It’s a popular theme park located at Sensosa Island. It features 7 themed zones: Hollywood, Madagascar, Far Far Away (my favorite), The Lost World, Ancient Egypt, Sci-Fi City, and New York. You probably want to spend a whole day to wander here because there’s just so much to see! Oh, and if you’re planning to ride CYCLON, make sure to do it last (trust me).

gardens by the bay.

One word: supertrees! If you appreciate plants and flowers, you would want to visit this place. You can also walk up in the elevated walkway and enjoy the view up on the OCBC Skyway (with fee). They also do daily lights and sound show called the Garden Rhapsody every night (for free!)!

flower dome.

cloud forest.

singapore zoo.

Fun day in the wild! Not only you can see animals here, they also do animal friendly shows like elephant show, Rainforest Fights Back and splash safari (plus a picture session with Pedro the sea lion after the show)

marina bay sands skypark.

It’s a very popular hotel in Singapore because of its structure and infinity pool all over Instagram though it is only open for the guests of the hotel. However, you could head up to the Skypark observation deck on the 57th level to get enjoy the stunning view it could offer.

helix bridge.

Short stop at helix bridge for an instagram worthy post!
