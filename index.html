Hi, I’m Chinkee!

I like learning new things and making fruit smoothies!

I’m a graduate of Bachelor of Science in Information Technology from University of the Assumption back in 2016. Landed my first job 2 months after as Test Process Engineer at a semiconductor company that manufactures memory modules. Crazy, right? 

“Do what you love.” , to some it can sound a bit hackneyed but it’s true. That’s why I make sure to try and learn things I’m passionate about (scroll down a little to know what I mean). I’m creative at heart and I express myself freely through designing. To pursue my dream career, I continuously learn to equip myself with skills and knowledge for professional growth.
